Wordpress Website



Alzheimer's and Music is a website that I developed using Wordpress. Studies show that music can help people affected by Alzheimer's or dementia in a variety of positive ways. This site was developed with the hope of assisting those suffering from Alzheimer's or dementia by providing a readily accessible selection of music broken down by decade from the 1940's through the 1970's.
